SECTION 4: LEGEND / STATUS DEFINITIONS
--------------------------------------
Balloon Valve Status (from file key):
- 1 = deflated
- 2 = ready to inflate at threshold (inhale)
- 3 = ready to inflate at threshold (exhale)
- 4 = inflated
- 5 = fault

Patient Switch:
- 0 = Disabled
- 1 = Enabled

Important practical meaning:
- In this project, status=4 is treated as breath-hold signal (label-positive
  condition in window logic).
- Status=1 indicates deflated/free-breathing-like condition.
