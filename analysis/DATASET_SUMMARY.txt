one or more recording sessions.

--------------

DATA COLUMNS
------------
Standard columns across all formats:
- Session Time (seconds) - Timestamp at ~50 Hz sampling rate
- Volume (liters) - Respiratory volume measurement
- Balloon Valve Status - 1=deflated, 2=ready inhale, 3=ready exhale, 4=inflated, 5=fault
- Patient Switch - Binary status (0/1)
- Gating Mode - "Automated" or "Manual Overide"
- Gating Status - Additional gating information
- Relay State - Relay control status
